Sales & Revenue Analysis

Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand regional performance, revenue trends, and profitable customer segments.

Approach

Performed data cleaning, SQL analysis, and Power BI dashboard development.

Key Insights

  • North region generated the highest revenue and growth YoY.
  • Repeat customers increased by 22%.
  • High-value customers contribute 43% of total revenue.

Tools Used

SQL, Power BI, Excel, Python (Pandas)
