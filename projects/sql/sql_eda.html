Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) in SQL

Project Overview

This Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project provides insights into global layoff trends by analyzing the cleaned layoffs dataset. SQL queries were used to identify patterns, trends, and anomalies across industries, countries, and time.

Dataset

layoffs-data-cleaned.csv

Key Insights

  • Tech companies represent the highest percentage of layoffs globally.
  • 2022 witnessed the largest spike in layoffs, especially among large-cap companies.
  • United States leads in total layoffs compared to all other countries combined.
  • Startups with 1–10 years of operation were the most affected.
  • Retail, Finance, and Transportation industries also showed steady increases.

Why This Matters

EDA helps identify major workforce shifts and allows companies to make strategic decisions based on real data rather than assumptions.
