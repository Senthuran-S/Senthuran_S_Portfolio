Excel Case Study: Bike Sales Dashboard

Customer Demographics • Buying Behaviour • Pivot Table Analytics

Dashboard Overview

This Excel dashboard provides insights into customer purchasing behavior for bike sales. Using Pivot Tables, charts, and interactive slicers, the dashboard highlights the relationships between demographics, income, commute distance, and purchasing likelihood.

Dataset

The dataset includes customer details such as age, gender, marital status, education level, income, commute distance, and whether they purchased a bike.

Bike Sales Dashboard.xlsx

Pivot Table Analysis

Pivot tables were used to segment buyers by:

  • Gender
  • Age Group
  • Education Level
  • Income Bracket
  • Commute Distance

This segmentation revealed meaningful customer patterns that helped shape the final dashboard insights.

Key Insights

  • 30–45 age group shows the highest likelihood of purchasing a bike.
  • Higher income groups tend to purchase more frequently.
  • Short commute distances correlate strongly with bike purchases.
  • Married individuals showed a slightly higher purchase rate.

Why This Matters

Businesses can use these insights to target high-value customer groups, improve marketing strategies, and better understand consumer buying trends.
