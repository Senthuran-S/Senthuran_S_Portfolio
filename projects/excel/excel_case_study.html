Objectives

  • Analyze demographic factors influencing bike purchases.
  • Identify customer groups with the highest conversion rates.
  • Use pivot tables to uncover patterns across income, age, distance, and region.
  • Visualize insights through clean and intuitive Excel dashboards.

Methodology

  1. Cleaned and structured raw customer dataset.
  2. Built multiple Pivot Tables for gender, income, commute distance, and purchase behavior.
  3. Designed Excel charts for dashboard visualization.
  4. Used slicers for dynamic filtering.
  5. Validated all calculations for data accuracy.

Conclusion

The Excel Bike Sales Dashboard demonstrates how effective data segmentation can uncover customer purchasing patterns. These insights help businesses target the right demographic, understand market demand, and optimize product marketing strategies.
