Bike Sales Dashboard (Excel)

Project Overview

This Excel-based dashboard provides sales insights for a bike company, including customer demographics, average income, commute distance, and total purchase behavior. Pivot tables and slicers enhance interactivity.

Dashboard File

View Bike Sales Dashboard →

Dataset

bike-sales-dataset.xlsx

Key Insights

  • Customers with higher income levels are more likely to purchase bikes.
  • Shorter commute distances correlate with higher purchase rates.
  • Suburban regions show strong sales performance.
  • Middle-age demographic groups purchase the most bikes.
  • Interactive filters allow segmented customer analysis.

Why This Matters

Excel dashboards remain a widely used tool in organizations, enabling fast and flexible business reporting for non-technical stakeholders.
