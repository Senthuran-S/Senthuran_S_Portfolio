Survey Data Dashboard (Power BI)

Project Overview

This dashboard visualizes a professional survey dataset to understand respondent demographics, career paths, work-life balance, learning preferences, and job satisfaction levels. The visual insights help organizations improve decision-making and workforce planning.

Dashboard File

View Survey Dashboard →

Dataset

survey-dataset.xlsx

Key Insights

  • Most respondents fall between ages 20–35, showing a young workforce.
  • Flexible working hours correlate with higher satisfaction scores.
  • Tech roles show the highest growth opportunities.
  • Majority of respondents prefer online courses for upskilling.
  • Salary satisfaction varies significantly by job role and experience level.

Why This Matters

Survey analytics help HR teams and leadership understand employee needs and adapt workplace policies to improve productivity and satisfaction.
