Objectives

  • Analyze demographic patterns across different job roles.
  • Understand salary distribution, career progression, and experience levels.
  • Identify factors influencing work-life balance and job satisfaction.
  • Provide data-driven insights for HR & business decision-making.

Methodology

  1. Cleaned and standardized survey fields for consistent reporting.
  2. Used DAX measures for KPIs like Average Salary & Satisfaction Index.
  3. Created bar charts, donut charts, KPIs, and line graphs for storytelling.
  4. Designed slicers for dynamic filtering across job roles & experience levels.
  5. Validated results for accuracy and business alignment.

Conclusion

This project highlights how Power BI can reveal deep workforce insights such as satisfaction drivers, salary trends, and career progression patterns. Organizations can use these findings to improve employee engagement, talent development, and long-term workforce strategy.
