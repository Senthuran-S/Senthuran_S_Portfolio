E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

End-to-end business intelligence solution built using Power BI, MySQL, DAX, and Star Schema modeling to analyze 9,694+ e-commerce transactions from 2014–2017.

Project Overview

This project involved building a complete self-service Power BI dashboard solution for an e-commerce business using the Superstore dataset. The objective was to replace static Excel reporting with an interactive analytics platform enabling leadership teams to monitor sales performance, identify profitability issues, and make data-driven decisions.

The project covered the full BI lifecycle including MySQL data preparation, Power BI data modeling, DAX measure creation, drill-through reporting, and multi-page dashboard development.

Data Model — Star Schema

A star schema data model was designed using the Superstore transactions table as the central fact table with a dedicated Date Table supporting time intelligence calculations.

MySQL → Power Query → Star Schema → DAX Measures → Interactive Dashboard → Business Insights

The Date Table was created using DAX CALENDAR and enriched with Year, Month, Quarter, and Year-Month columns. A one-to-many relationship was established between Date Table[Date] and Superstore[Order Date].

DAX Measures & Time Intelligence

Multiple DAX measures were developed covering KPIs, profitability analysis, and time intelligence reporting.

  • Total Sales
  • Total Profit
  • Profit Margin %
  • Total Orders
  • MTD Sales
  • YTD Sales
  • Previous Year Sales
  • YoY Growth %

Executive Summary Dashboard

The Executive Summary page provides leadership with a complete business overview including sales, profit, margin performance, category contribution, and regional analysis.

West region generated the highest profit while Central region showed weak profitability despite strong sales volume.

Drill-Through Navigation

Drill-through functionality was implemented enabling users to right-click categories and navigate directly into detailed sub-category analysis pages.

Category Detail Analysis

The Category Detail page identifies high-sales but low-profit sub-categories using scatter plots, profitability tables, and sales contribution analysis.

Tables generated strong sales volume but produced negative profitability, revealing aggressive discount-driven losses.

Conditional Formatting & Profitability Insights

Conditional formatting with red-to-green gradients was applied to Profit Margin % values enabling instant identification of loss-making sub-categories.

Customer Profitability Analysis

Customer-level analysis revealed that high revenue customers were not always profitable customers.

155 customers generated negative margins. Sean Miller was the top revenue customer but produced negative profitability.

Sales Trends & Time Intelligence

Time intelligence reporting was developed using MTD, YTD, Previous Year Sales, and YoY Growth measures to identify seasonal sales patterns and yearly growth trends.

Key Business Insights

Central Region Efficiency Problem

Central region generated significantly higher sales than South but lower profit, indicating operational inefficiencies.

Furniture Margin Crisis

Tables and Bookcases produced negative margins despite strong sales performance.

Customer Profitability Imbalance

19.5% of customers generated negative profit margins.

Seasonal Sales Trends

September to November consistently produced the highest sales across all four years.
